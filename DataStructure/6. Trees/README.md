### Binary Search Tree (BST)

It's called **ordered** or **sorted binary tree**
|Algorithm |Average | Worst case |
|--|--|--|
|**Space** | O(_n_) | O(_n_) |
|**Search** | O(log _n_) | O(_n_) |
|**Insert** | O(log _n_) | O(_n_) |
|**Delete** | O(log _n_) | O(_n_) |
